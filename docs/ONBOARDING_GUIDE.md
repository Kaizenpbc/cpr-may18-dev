# CPR Training System — New Customer Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the CPR Training Management System. This guide walks a new organization through everything needed to go from first login to a completed, invoiced training course.

---

## Step 1 — Log In for the First Time

Your system administrator will send you a username and temporary password.

1. Go to **https://cpr.kpbc.ca**
2. Enter your username and password
3. You will be prompted to set a new password on first login
   - Minimum 8 characters, must include a number and a capital letter

> **Your role**: Organization users log in to the **Organization Portal**. Your view is scoped to your organization only — you will not see other organizations' data.

---

## Step 2 — Understand Your Dashboard

After logging in you land on the Organization Dashboard:

| Section | What it shows |
|---------|--------------|
| **Upcoming Courses** | Confirmed classes scheduled for your organization |
| **Pending Requests** | Course requests not yet scheduled |
| **Recent Activity** | Latest status changes |
| **Billing Summary** | Outstanding invoices at a glance |

---

## Step 3 — Request Your First Course

1. Click **Course Requests** in the left navigation
2. Click **New Request**
3. Fill in the form:

   | Field | Notes |
   |-------|-------|
   | **Course Type** | e.g. Basic Life Support (BLS), CPR-C, First Aid |
   | **Preferred Date(s)** | One or more dates that work for you |
   | **Expected Students** | Approximate headcount (affects instructor assignment) |
   | **Location** | Full address where the class will be held |
   | **Notes** | Any special requirements (AED on site, accessible venue, etc.) |

4. Click **Submit Request**

Your request will appear as **Pending** while the course admin reviews it and assigns an instructor.

---

## Step 4 — Track Your Request

Course requests move through these statuses:

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Pending** | Submitted — waiting for scheduling |
| **Confirmed** | Instructor and date are locked in |
| **Completed** | Class has been delivered |
| **Invoiced** | Invoice has been generated |

You will receive an email notification at each status change. You can also check the status at any time under **Course Requests**.

---

## Step 5 — Add Students to a Confirmed Course

Once your course is **Confirmed**:

1. Go to **My Courses**
2. Click on the confirmed course
3. Click **Manage Students**
4. Add students one by one (name + email), or upload a CSV file:

   ```
   First Name, Last Name, Email
   Jane, Smith, jane@example.com
   John, Doe, john@example.com
   ```

5. Click **Save**

> Students do not need system accounts. The roster is used for attendance and certificate records only.

---

## Step 6 — After the Class

The instructor takes attendance on the day of the class. Once the course is marked **Completed** by the course admin:

- You can view attendance results under **My Courses → [Course] → View Results**
- The course is sent to Accounting for invoicing

---

## Step 7 — Pay Your Invoice

When an invoice is generated you will see it under **Billing**:

1. Click **Billing** in the navigation
2. Find the invoice (status: **Pending**)
3. Review the amount and due date
4. Click **Record Payment** and enter:
   - Amount paid
   - Payment method (cheque, e-transfer, etc.)
   - Reference number
5. Click **Submit**

Accounting will verify and mark the invoice as **Paid**.

---

## Managing Your Users

If your organization has multiple staff members who need access, contact your system administrator to have additional accounts created. Each user can be assigned the **orguser** role, which limits access to your organization's data only.

---

## Getting Help

| Who to contact | For what |
|---------------|---------|
| **Course Admin** | Scheduling, instructor questions, rescheduling |
| **Accounting** | Invoice disputes, payment questions |
| **System Administrator** | Login issues, new user accounts, technical problems |

For urgent issues, contact your designated system administrator directly.

---

## Quick Reference

| Task | Where to go |
|------|------------|
| Request a new course | Course Requests → New Request |
| Check course status | Course Requests or My Courses |
| Add students to a class | My Courses → [Course] → Manage Students |
| View invoices | Billing |
| Record a payment | Billing → [Invoice] → Record Payment |
| Update your password | Profile (top-right corner) |
